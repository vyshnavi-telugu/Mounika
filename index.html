MEANING OF NAMES

MOUNIKA
pure silence and innocence, It can also be interpreted to mean "inner peace" or "tranquility". The name is often used for girls and is of Sanskrit and Bengali origin.
HARSHINI
The name Harshini typically means "happiness," "joy," or "delight." The name often suggests a person who is full of cheerfulness and brings joy to others.
VAISHNAVI
The name Vaishnavi generally signifies a "devotee of Vishnu" or "one who is beloved by Vishnu". It is a female name of Indian origin, often associated with divine qualities and devotion to the Hindu deity Vishnu.
